Our Story, Our Craft.

Gradient Grounds Coffee Co. began with a simple vision: to create a space where exceptional coffee meets community and creativity. Our journey started in a small roastery, fueled by a passion for ethically sourced beans and the art of the perfect brew. We believe every cup tells a story, and ours is one of dedication, quality, and connection.

Our Vision & Aim

Our aim is to inspire moments of joy and connection, one cup at a time. We strive to be more than just a coffee shop; we are a community hub where creativity flourishes, conversations thrive, and every visitor feels at home. We are committed to sustainable practices, partnering with farmers who share our values, and continuously innovating our craft to deliver unparalleled flavor.


We envision a future where Gradient Grounds is renowned for its exceptional coffee, welcoming atmosphere, and positive impact on both local and global communities. Our commitment extends from seed to cup, ensuring quality and ethical responsibility at every step.

The Journey

Born from a dream in 2018, Gradient Grounds began as a small, experimental pop-up, driven by a desire to share unique coffee experiences. Our founder, Alex Chen, traveled extensively, learning from master roasters and discovering rare bean varieties. The name "Gradient Grounds" reflects this journey of discovery and the gradual refinement of our craft, evolving through various blends and brewing techniques.


From a humble beginning, we grew, gathering a loyal following who appreciated our dedication to quality and our innovative approach to flavor profiles. Each milestone, from opening our first physical store to launching our signature blends, has been a step forward in perfecting the Gradient Grounds experience. Today, we continue to honor our roots while embracing the future of coffee.

Meet Our Passionate Team

Sasi Kumar, Founder & Head Roaster

Sasi's love affair with coffee began over a decade ago, leading to a relentless pursuit of the perfect bean and the most exquisite roast. With a background in culinary arts and a deep understanding of flavor chemistry, Sasi personally oversees the sourcing and roasting process, ensuring every batch meets Gradient Grounds' high standards. Their vision and passion are the heart of our brand.
